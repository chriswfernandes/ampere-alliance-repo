Method

How the scan runs — and how it scales to 200+

01

Source

Corporate registries, trade associations, certification & bonding lists, LinkedIn, and regional news — swept for E&I firms in target geographies.

02

Enrich

Each firm matched to segment, revenue band, ownership tenure, and public succession cues, then deduplicated against the portfolio.

03

Score

Six-factor model applied — the same weights you set above — turning a list into a ranked, comparable pipeline.

04

Rank

Composite score, tier, and territory density surface the handful worth a partner's time first.

05

Stage

Top tier routes to operational diligence and a templated Day 1–100 onboarding — the repeatable part.
